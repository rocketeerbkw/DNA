White Girl

Monday 10 March 9pm

This provocative and emotive drama from the Bafta Award-winning writer of Sex Traffic tells the story of the clashing of cultures that occurs when a white family relocates from Leeds to a wholly Asian community in Bradford.

Abi Morgan's compelling film is told from the per- spective of 11-year-old Leah, played by newcomer Holly Kenny, whose world is turned upside down when her mum Debbie (Bleak House's Anna Max- well Martin) moves the family to Bradford to escape the fallout from her recent break-up.

Tensions arise when Leah befriends her young Asian neighbour Yasmin and discovers that her new friend's culture isn't so alien after all. Leah is soon seeking sanctuary in the Muslim world away from the pain and strife at home. But this innocent fascination soon turns sour for Debbie when her daughter comes home wearing a hijab, and the family's violent reaction has explosive conse- quences for everyone.
