Commenting for Everyone

Enabling user participation and conversation, BBC Discuss is a straight forward social component supported by a full range of powerful moderation tools.

Encouraging discussion, it can be quickly implemented with large web applications or small carefully crafted sites. Created by a team that has been at the centre of user generated content propositions in the BBC for more than 6 years, BBC Discuss can also offer advice and guidance on fostering and maintaining online communities.

Features

  • Plug and Play Functionality
  • Complete integration with SSO and soon, Identity
  • Enhanced user experience with the BBC Glow Javascript library
  • Clean and accessible HTML with appropriate Microformats
  • Supported by an experienced Moderation team and a full suite of tools for site owners
